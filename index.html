We will discuss some of the main causes of climate change on Earth!

School bus

Bus travel releases gases such as carbon dioxide as waste products. Carbon dioxides traps heat which is reradiated as infrared waves by the Earth's surface.

Food Waste

Food which is wasted especially in school. Decomposition of food generates greenhouse gases such as methane, CH4. Also, the transportation of food by means of transport releases greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.

Electricity dissipation

Inefficient printers and light bulbs have a poor energy efficiency leading to greater energy waste. This can be solved by using efficient machines and light bulbs such as LEDs.
